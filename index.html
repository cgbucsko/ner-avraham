What do retired lawyers, doctors, dentists and businessmen have in common with young men who have spent their lives toiling in full time Torah learning? Anyone who has set foot in the Ner Avraham Community Kollel of Rechavia knows the answer to this question. There, the delight of Torah learning is a common thread which bridges the gap of generations and backgrounds.​

Whether it be through the morning classes in Chumash, Navi and Mishna, or the one-on-one learning opportunities with the Kollel members, the residents of Rechavia/ Shaarei Chessed and beyond find Ner Avraham a fresh water source to quench their thirst for Torah learning. Please become a partner in this wonderful Kiddush Hashem – building lives and warming hearts.
